Insight Meditation Center

Everyone Welcome

The Insight Meditation Center is a community-based urban meditation center for the practice of Vipassana or Insight meditation guided by Gil Fronsdal and Andrea Fella.

We are a non-residential center in Redwood City, California dedicated to the study and practice of Buddhist teachings.
